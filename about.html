The craft

Crafted by conviction.

No distillate. No cutting agents. Our live rosin is pressed from fresh-frozen flower using nothing but ice, heat, and pressure — so what ends up in the tin tastes like the plant it came from.

  • •Fresh-frozen flower. Harvested at peak and frozen immediately to lock in flavor and aroma.
  • •Clean press. Ice-water and heat extract the rosin — zero chemicals.
  • •Small-batch infusion. Pressed into gummies, pre-rolls, and carts in limited runs.
